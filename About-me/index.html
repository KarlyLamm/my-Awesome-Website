Personal Journey to Software development

My goal is to become a software engineer. While I could say that I found programming, the truth is that programming found a way to me. In a transitional period for me, programming came in at just the right time. While I believe I was meant to work in the tech field, I have not always seen my carear field in it. I attended a Pharmacy school for several years in pursuit of a PharmD; however, I have always been drawn to the worlds of math, science, and computers. Growing up in a rapidly developing tech world, I have always been fascinated with the idea of creation and electronics. From my toddler ages of attempting to build non-functional robots from soup cans with the imagination that they were programmed, to middle-school days of downloading programs on the home computer to make stick-figure animation games. When I first typed the words "Hello, World" into the console of my application, I knew I was hooked.
